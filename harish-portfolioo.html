About me

Turning raw data into decisions

I genuinely enjoy digging into data and figuring out what's actually going on beneath the surface — the kind of question that starts with "why did this number move?" and doesn't end until I've found the actual reason. With hands-on experience in Python, SQL, and database management across real projects, I've spent a lot of time working with data that wasn't clean or ready to plot, and getting it there.
